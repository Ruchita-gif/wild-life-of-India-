Wildlife by States in India

Rajasthan

Rajasthan is famous for the Ranthambore National Park, home to Bengal tigers and diverse wildlife.

Kerala

Kerala's Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary is known for elephants, tigers, and rich biodiversity.

Assam

Assam is home to Kaziranga National Park, famous for the one-horned rhinoceros and other wildlife.

Uttarakhand

Uttarakhand's Hemis National Park shelters the elusive snow leopard and other Himalayan species.
