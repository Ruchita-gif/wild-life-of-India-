Welcome to the Wildlife of India

Discover the rich and diverse wildlife of India, home to some of the world's most magnificent animals and natural habitats.

Explore India's Wildlife

India is known for its incredible biodiversity, ranging from majestic tigers and elephants to colorful birds and reptiles. Our website provides information about the various species, their habitats, and the states where you can find them.

About Us

We are storytellers, conservationists, and digital explorers united by our passion for India's wilderness

The Heartbeat of the Jungle

Jungle Pulse was born from a midnight encounter with a Bengal tiger in the forests of Bandhavgarh. That moment of connection with wild India sparked our mission to bring the jungle's magic to the digital world.

Our team combines wildlife biologists, tribal storytellers, and digital artists to create experiences that honor both scientific truth and ancient wisdom.

We believe technology should not separate us from nature, but rather deepen our connection to it. Every element of Jungle Pulse is designed to evoke the sights, sounds, and soul of India's wilderness.

Types of Animals in India

Mammals

India is home to a wide variety of mammals including the Bengal tiger, Indian elephant, Indian leopard, and the Asiatic lion.

Birds

India is home to over 1,300 bird species, including the magnificent Indian peacock and endangered species.

Reptiles

Discover India's diverse reptilian fauna, from the gharial and king cobra to various turtle species.

Wildlife by States in India

Rajasthan

Rajasthan is famous for the Ranthambore National Park, home to Bengal tigers and diverse wildlife.

Kerala

Kerala's Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary is known for elephants, tigers, and rich biodiversity.

Assam

Assam is home to Kaziranga National Park, famous for the one-horned rhinoceros and other wildlife.

Uttarakhand

Uttarakhand's Hemis National Park shelters the elusive snow leopard and other Himalayan species.
